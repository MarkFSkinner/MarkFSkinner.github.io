About me

Hi, I'm Mark Skinner, a self-taught Front-End Web Developer with knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, React, Redux, Bootstrap, Git, Sass, Responsive Design, Browser Developer Tools, Node.js, npm, etc.

My formal education includes an Honors Bachelor of Commerce degree from McMaster University. I previously spent 6 years working in the insurance industry in Waterloo, Ontario. Feeling that my desire for creative problem solving, analytical thinking and personal growth was not being fully realized in my current profession, I decided to explore a new path and delve into the world of software and web development. Using numerous online resources, including freeCodeCamp, Udacity & Codecademy, I have built a strong foundation of knowledge, skills & experience in building web sites and web apps. Please view my portfolio below and visit my github profile to see these skills put into action.
